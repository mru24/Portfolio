About Me

Hi, my name is Val. Web development and programing has been my secret passion for many years, since I put my hands on ZX Spectrum computer. When I got Amiga 600 and finally Amiga 1200 computer I could create some games and apps using Amos and C languages.

I started web development when I bought my first PC in 1996. Today my smartwatch is faster than that computer but it was very exciting being able to create something. Obviously it was nothing like todays web development. Tables, inline styling. I was using Dreamweaver without any deeper knowledge of technology.

Then it was family, work, home. There was no time for a hobby which at the beginning of 21 century wasn't giving me any money. Today I am trying go get back on track. I spent last 20 months (today is 29 May 2018) learnig stuff and my plan is to become Junior Web Developer specializing in Front End responsive development. Have I got a chance? Take a look at my works and let me know. My goal is to learn all cutting-edge technologies required to build modern, advanced and well looking web pages.

It's just a matter of time..
